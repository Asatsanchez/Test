üí° Knowledge Base & FAQs

Quick answers to common questions

How do I upload a tax certificate for a supplier? +

Steps:

  1. Navigate to: Procurement ‚Üí Suppliers ‚Üí Manage Suppliers
  2. Search for and select the supplier
  3. Click on "Tax Details" tab
  4. Click "Add Certificate"
  5. Enter certificate details (type, number, valid from/to dates)
  6. Upload supporting document (PDF)
  7. Click "Save and Close"

Validation: System will automatically validate certificate number format and expiry date.

What happens if a certificate expires during an invoice approval cycle? +

System Behavior:

  • System checks certificate validity at time of tax calculation (not invoice entry)
  • If certificate expires before payment, system applies standard (higher) rate
  • Holds are automatically placed on invoices requiring certificate renewal
  • Notification sent to tax team and supplier contact

Resolution: Upload renewed certificate, then release hold manually or through workflow.

Can I manually override the calculated WHT amount? +

Yes, if enabled: Manual override capability must be enabled in Tax Options by administrator.

Process:

  1. Open the invoice in edit mode
  2. Navigate to tax details section
  3. Click "Override" on tax line
  4. Enter reason code and new amount
  5. Save (override logs for audit trail)

Best Practice: Use override sparingly and document reason in notes field.

How do I generate the quarterly WHT return report? +

Navigation:

  1. Go to: Tools ‚Üí Reports and Analytics
  2. Search: "Withholding Tax Return Report"
  3. Click "Create" to schedule the report
  4. Enter parameters:
    • Business Unit
    • Reporting Period (Quarter)
    • Country/Tax Regime
    • Output Format (PDF/Excel)
  5. Submit and monitor in Scheduled Processes

Output: Report includes all WHT transactions for the period with supplier details, amounts, and certificate references.

What should I do if tax is not calculating on an invoice? +

Troubleshooting Checklist:

  1. Check Supplier Setup: Verify supplier has tax classification and WHT is enabled at site level
  2. Verify Certificate: Ensure certificate is attached and status is "Valid"
  3. Review Tax Rules: Check that determination rules match invoice attributes (service type, amount, date)
  4. Check Invoice Attributes: Verify tax classification code is populated on invoice line
  5. Validate Tax Regime: Confirm tax regime is active and effective for transaction date

Still Not Working? Contact your tax administrator or submit a help desk ticket with invoice number.
